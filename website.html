WhatisWaste ???

Waste is any substance which is discarded
after primary use, it is worthless, defective
and of no use.

AgriculturalWaste

Agriculture is the art of cultivating the soil, producing crops and raising livestock.

Agricultural Residues

It is the plant part left after obtaining the useable portions. Much of this is used as animal feed. It can also be used for making natural manure.

Bagasse



It is the plant residue (as of sugarcane) after extracting the sugarcane juice. They are used as firewood or in paper industry.




Pesticides and Fertilizers

They collect into the soil and are washed off with irrigation and rain water causing water pollution. It is advisable to minimize their use.

Animal Wastes




They include cowdung and other faecal matter which are used in making manure.






IndustrialWaste

Mining-Operations

Huge Quantities of wastes are produced while extracting and processing ores. Such waste is usually called mine-tailing. Mine tailing mixed with other materials is popularly used for making tiles.

Cement Industries

Solid Wastes - They are either used in construction activities or dumped in landfills.
Liqud Wastes - They are treated to remove harmful substances and are then released into water bodies.
Gaseous Wastes - They include flyash (fine solid particles of non-combustible ash) which is used in making bricks, roofing sheets, concrete etc.

Oil Refineries

Gaseous Wastes - They are cleaned by passing through cleaners and purified part is released into the athmosphere.

Liquid Wastes - They are processed and suitably disposed.

Construction Units

These produce huge quantities of broken bricks, waste stones, pebbles, wood waste, etc. These are mostly dumped in landfills.
